About Passport Seva

Passport Seva is an initiative by the Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India, to deliver passport-related services in a safe, secure, and citizen-friendly manner.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, transparent, and accessible passport services to Indian citizens across the globe.

Nationwide Network

We operate through over 77 Passport Seva Kendras (PSKs) and 93 Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSKs).

24/7 Assistance

Our helpline and support services are available 24/7 to assist applicants with queries and issues.

Digital India Aligned

Fully integrated with the Digital India initiative for seamless online applications, tracking, and updates.

At Passport Seva, we are committed to simplifying the passport issuance process and ensuring timely delivery of services to every citizen.
